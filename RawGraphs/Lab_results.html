Contour Plot

The data used are from the Energy Information Administration's 2015 Residential Energy Consumption Survey (RECS) which is a nationally representative sample of housing units. We only used 2 columns "Number of rooms" and "Households with a stove using fuel". Because it's a large dataset, I chose to visualize the distribution and correlation of the data using a contour plot that shows estimated density of point clouds.
